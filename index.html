A Tanzanian Brand with a Big Vision.

Puma Harvest was born from a simple belief that Tanzania's agricultural riches deserve to be shared with pride.

We started with Sasso chicken because we believe in doing one thing exceptionally well. Sasso is a heritage breed prized for its exceptional flavour, firm texture, and community partnership. We are here to build a premium food brand that honors where we come from and the kind of brand we're building.

Today, it's Sasso chicken. Tomorrow, it's honey, jams, spices, teas, and other products that showcase the best of our country. This is just the beginning.
